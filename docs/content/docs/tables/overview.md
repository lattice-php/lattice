---
title: Tables
description: Eloquent-backed listings with columns, sorting, filtering, pagination, and row and bulk actions.
---

A table is an Eloquent-backed listing. You declare columns — such as `TextColumn` and `StackColumn` — and Lattice applies sorting, filtering, and pagination against the database, fetching rows from the table's endpoint. Filters support text, presence, and comparison operators per column, and rows and selections can carry actions.
